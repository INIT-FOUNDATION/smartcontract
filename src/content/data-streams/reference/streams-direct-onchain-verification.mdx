<Aside type="caution" title="Verifying data onchain">
  Onchain data verification means confirming that the onchain report corresponds to the one signed by the Chainlink DON.
  It is the responsibility of the application contract(s) to validate whether the report's data is suitable for
  executing any intended actions.
</Aside>

The primary on-chain interaction occurs between the `IVerifierProxy.sol` contract and your protocol's client contract. `IVerifierProxy.sol` serves as the sole interface for the client contract to validate reports, with other underlying contracts managing verification specifics.
